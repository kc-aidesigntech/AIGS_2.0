Community at Heart

We would like to invite you in as we seek to serve the Anderson Island community's most fundamental needs, and work diligently every day to better ourselves. We strive to be the place where locals and visitors gather over coffee or a warm meal, share a friendly chat, and find what they need.

Can't find what you need? Just ask! We may just have it or it may be on the next pickup and we'll tell you when we'll have it next! We would be pleased to provide you with what you need, and you'll be surprised to find out how far we'll go to make it happen!
